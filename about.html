About Eternal City Ministries

Rooted in Christ, reaching every nation, tribe, and tongue with hope, redemption, and eternal life.

Vision Statement

"Eternal City Ministries International exists to bring nations into the eternal life of Christ, empowering them to experience immortality through His transformative Word and power."

Mission Statement

"Transforming nations through Christ's eternal life. To reach every nation, tribe, and tongue with the Gospel of Jesus Christ, bringing hope, redemption, and eternal life to all people."

Core Values

Christ-Centered

Jesus Christ is the foundation of our ministry.

Biblical Truth

Scripture is our authoritative guide.

Compassionate Service

Serving with empathy, kindness, and humility.

Prayer

Prioritizing intercession and spiritual warfare.

Innovation

Creative methods for Gospel outreach.

Community & Integrity

Building believers; practicing transparency and moral excellence.

Cultural Relevance

Sharing the Gospel in culturally sensitive ways.

Holistic Transformation

Addressing spiritual, emotional, and physical needs.
