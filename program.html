Our Programs

Join us for worship, prayer, and spiritual growth.

Sunday Service

Every Sunday
09:00 AM - 03:00 PM CAT

Join us for a powerful worship experience and biblical teaching. We gather to celebrate Christ's eternal life and grow together as a community of believers.

  • Worship: Inspirational music and praise
  • Teaching: Biblical instruction and revelation
  • Prayer: Intercession for nations and individuals
  • Fellowship: Community connection and support

Mid-Week Service

Every Wednesday
5:00pm CAT

A midweek gathering focused on prayer, spiritual warfare, and deeper discipleship. This intimate setting allows us to seek God's face and intercede for the nations.

  • Prayer Focus: Intercession and spiritual warfare
  • Discipleship: Deeper study of Scripture
  • Testimony: Sharing God's faithfulness
  • Ministry: Prophetic words and encouragement

Eternal Experience

Annual Last Friday of October
Time: 7:00 PM (local)

A powerful annual gathering — join us the last Friday of October for worship, prophetic ministry, and a move of God's presence.

  • Worship: Extended praise and adoration
  • Encounter: Prophetic ministry and testimony
  • Global Focus: Intercession for nations
